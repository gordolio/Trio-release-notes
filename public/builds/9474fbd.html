Sensor lifetime indicator on glucose bobble

  • A colored arc around the glucose bobble drains clockwise to show the sensor's remaining life.
  • A short tag below the bobble shows time left or a status word like "warming up" or "sensor expired".
  • Warmup and sensor-failed states hide the arc and show the glucose as a muted "– –".
  • G7 sensors now count down through the grace period so the bobble's time tag matches the arc.
  • The glucose simulator adds a Sensor Lifecycle picker so you can preview these sensor states without hardware.
