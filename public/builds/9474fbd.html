Highlights

Adds a CGM sensor lifetime indicator to the home-screen glucose bobble (outer arc driven by cgmLifecycleProgress plus a small status/remaining-time tag) supporting six sensor states (normal, expiring soon, warmup, calibration required, expired, sensor failed). Extends HomeStateModel.sensorExpiresAt to resolve expiration via manager read, reverse-derivation from activatedAt + percentComplete, and a percent-label fallback. Simulator gains a “Sensor Lifecycle Scenario” picker that emits cgmStatusHighlight and cgmLifecycleProgress and can simulate non-active scenarios. UI/layout changes overlay the tag on the bobble, scale/compact the bobble correctly, provide a compact imageName fallback for non-warmup errors, and mute the glucose number for warmup/failure states.

Users will see a new sensor lifetime visual on the home glucose bobble: an outer progress arc showing sensor life and a concise tag with time remaining or a status word (e.g., “warming up”, “22h left”, “sensor expired”). Warmup/failure states alter presentation (arc hidden; glucose muted). The simulator UI exposes all six lifecycle scenarios for testing. NOTE: this implementation expects updated CGMBLEKit support (fork/branch) and xDrip4iOS lifecycle-like data is not yet shipped; further integration/compatibility work may be needed.
