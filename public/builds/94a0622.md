## Highlights

- **Core Data performance and stability fixes**
  - Background data operations now use fresh Core Data contexts to avoid stale data and reduce races.

  - TDD (total daily dose) average calculations run faster due to aggregate queries.
  - Pump-event deduplication now uses timestamp plus type so a bolus and a temp basal at the same time are both kept.

- **Acknowledge pump alerts when dismissed**
  - Dismissing the in-app alert banner or swiping away a notification also acknowledges pump alerts at the device.
  - This stops pod or pump beeps when you dismiss their alerts.
  - Tapping or swiping still snoozes alerts for 15 minutes and removes on-screen presentations.
  - Alerts from Trio internals (CGM/algorithm) are not affected by this change.
  - [View source](https://github.com/nightscout/Trio/pull/1285)
- **Swipe to end snooze**
  - End an active snooze by swiping the snooze row to the left.

  - The snooze sheet footer shows a hand symbol and text instructing to swipe left.
  - The previous Undo Snooze button has been replaced by the swipe action.
  - [View source](https://github.com/nightscout/Trio/pull/1293)
