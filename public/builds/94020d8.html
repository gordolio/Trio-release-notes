Highlights

Exclude temp target presets from the main chart and re-filter fetched results with a fresh "now". The NSPredicate.tempTargetsForMainChart predicate was changed to require isPreset == false for scheduled items (Model/Helper/TempTargetStored+Helper.swift). A new TempTargetStored.isVisibleInChart computed property was added to represent which temp targets should be drawn (running or non-preset future scheduled items). Home.StateModel.updateTempTargetsFromController now filters the controller's fetchedObjects with .isVisibleInChart before assigning to the chart model. Tests were added to verify the predicate behavior and chart visibility rules.

Users should no longer see newly saved temp-target presets (never activated) appear in the main chart. Cancelled/expired temp targets that previously remained drawn until the app was backgrounded should also stop being shown because the fetched results are re-filtered with a fresh date before reaching the chart. The change is limited to chart visibility logic for temp targets and adds tests to cover the behavior.
