Fixes

Unifies WatchConnectivity incoming paths into a single handler and adds freshness and monotonicity guards: centralizes didReceiveMessage/didReceiveUserInfo handling, drops snapshots older than ~15 minutes, persists and checks a latest-date to deduplicate non-new snapshots, stamps outgoing phone-sent snapshots with Date() to ensure newer timestamps, and preserves ack/recommended-bolus handling outside the staleness gate.

Incoming watch-state payloads older than ~15 minutes will be ignored (reducing processing of backlogged/stale messages); duplicate or non-new snapshots will be skipped via the persisted monotonic timestamp; phone-initiated re-pushes are stamped with the current time so they are less likely to be silenced. Ack and recommended-bolus messages continue to be handled without the staleness gate. Runtime verification/QA is recommended.
